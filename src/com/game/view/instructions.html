Instructions

  • You must enter a verb and a noun to direct the caterpillar. Ex. shield self
  • Level one goal is to eat leaf and avoid enemies. Navigate with go [direction].
  • Eat leaves and attack foes you can defeat.
  • At level seven, fight the boss (squirrel) to save your mate.
  • To win, defeat the squirrel and then go to the tree.
  • For easy game play enter go godmode
  • Adjust volume with volume [up/down]
  • Get help with help, help all, help defenses, or help [defense].
  • To battle a predator:
    1. Enter battle mode: attack [predator]
    2. Bring on the damage: use [defense type]
